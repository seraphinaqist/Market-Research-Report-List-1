<p><p>The flow cytometry instruments market is primarily segmented into cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are designed for the quantitative analysis of cell populations, measuring various parameters such as size, complexity, and fluorescence intensity. In contrast, cell sorters provide advanced capabilities to isolate specific cell types from a mixed population based on predefined criteria, allowing for further analysis or experimentation. Both types are essential for research and clinical applications in fields like immunology, oncology, and cell biology.</p></p>
