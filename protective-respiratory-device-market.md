<p><ul><li>Air Purifying Respirators (APR）</li><li>Supplied Air Respirators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protective respiratory device market primarily comprises two main types: Air Purifying Respirators (APR) and Supplied Air Respirators. APRs filter contaminants from the air, using mechanisms like particulate filters and chemical cartridges to provide clean air to the wearer. They are suitable for areas with lower pollution levels. In contrast, Supplied Air Respirators deliver breathable air from an external source, ensuring safety in highly contaminated environments, making them critical for industrial applications and emergency situations where air quality is compromised.</p></p>
