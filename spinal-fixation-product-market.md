<p><ul><li>Spinal Fusion</li><li>Non-fusion Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal fixation product market is primarily divided into two categories: spinal fusion and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products are designed to stabilize and join segments of the spine, promoting bone growth and preventing movement between vertebrae, often used in cases of spinal disorders and injuries. Non-fusion products, on the other hand, provide stabilization without permanently merging vertebrae, maintaining flexibility while addressing issues like degenerative disc disease. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes through improved spinal support and mobility.</p></p>
