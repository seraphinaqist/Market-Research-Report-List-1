<p><p>The Home Non-Invasive Ventilator market for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) comprises two primary types: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators provide a constant airflow pressure, suitable for patients with stable breathing patterns. In contrast, Bilevel Ventilators offer two pressure settings—one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation—making them ideal for patients with varying respiratory needs. Both types support effective management of COPD, enhancing patients' quality of life through improved breathing support at home.</p></p>
