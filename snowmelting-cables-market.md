<p><ul><li>Constant Temperature</li><li>Temperature-limited</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snowmelting cables market is categorized into two main types: constant temperature and temperature-limited cables. Constant temperature cables maintain a steady heat output, ensuring reliable snow and ice melting regardless of environmental conditions. In contrast, temperature-limited cables adjust their heat output based on the ambient temperature, providing energy efficiency by reducing heat in milder conditions while still melting snow effectively in colder weather. Both types serve to enhance safety and accessibility in snowy environments.</p></p>
