<p><p>Aluminum foam is a lightweight and versatile material used in construction, available in two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell aluminum foam features interconnected voids that enhance sound absorption and reduce weight, making it suitable for architectural applications and noise insulation. In contrast, closed cell aluminum foam consists of sealed cells, providing superior strength, insulation, and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for structural supports and impact-resistant applications in various construction projects.</p></p>
