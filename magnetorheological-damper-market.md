<p><p>The magnetorheological damper market is categorized into active and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize an external control system to adjust the viscosity of the fluid in real-time, enabling precise control over damping characteristics for optimal performance. In contrast, semi-active dampers also adjust fluid properties but rely on passive elements to respond to changing conditions, offering a balance between performance and simplicity. Both types enhance vehicle stability and comfort, making them valuable in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications.</p></p>
