<p><p>The Polyether Diamines market is segmented into two primary types: PPG (Polypropylene Glycol) Backbone and PEG (Polyethylene Glycol) Backbone. PPG Backbone polyether diamines are known for their excellent flexibility and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for applications in coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, PEG Backbone polyether diamines typically exhibit lower reactivity and are favored for applications requiring better thermal stability and hydrophilicity, such as in personal care products and pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
