<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed Air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Anti-icing System Market encompasses various types of ice protection technologies. Pneumatic de-icing boots utilize inflatable rubber surfaces to remove ice. Electro-thermal systems employ electrical heating elements to melt ice on surfaces. Electro-mechanical systems focus on mechanically removing ice using moving parts. TKS ice protection uses a glycol-based fluid spread over wing surfaces. Bleed air systems draw hot air from engines to prevent ice formation. Passive systems rely on aircraft design features to minimize ice accumulation without active measures.</p></p>
