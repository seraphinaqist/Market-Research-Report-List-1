<p><strong>The Division Multiplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM</li><li>WDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Division Multiplexer market primarily includes two types: Frequency Division Multiplexing (FDM) and Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM). FDM divides the available bandwidth into multiple channels, allowing simultaneous transmission of different signals over a single medium, primarily used in radio and telecommunications. In contrast, WDM operates within fiber optic networks, utilizing different wavelengths of light to transmit multiple data streams concurrently. Both technologies enhance communication efficiency and capacity in various industries, including telecommunications, broadcasting, and data centers.</p></p>
