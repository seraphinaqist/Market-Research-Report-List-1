<p><p>The Household Atomizer market comprises three main types: Pneumatic Atomizers, Ultrasonic Atomizers, and Net Atomizers. Pneumatic Atomizers use air pressure to create a fine mist, making them effective for larger areas. Ultrasonic Atomizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to produce a fine spray, ideal for essential oils and humidification. Net Atomizers, often employing mesh technology, create a mist by pushing liquid through tiny openings, providing a consistent and efficient delivery. Each type serves distinct purposes in household applications, enhancing comfort and convenience.</p></p>
