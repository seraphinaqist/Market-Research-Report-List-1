<p><p>The loan services market includes various types of financing options catering to different borrower needs. Conventional loans are typically offered by banks and require good credit without government backing. Conforming loans meet specific guidelines set by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac, ensuring lower rates. FHA loans are government-insured, making them accessible for low-to-moderate income borrowers. Private money loans are sourced from individuals or investors, often with less stringent criteria. Hard money loans are short-term, asset-based loans mainly used for real estate investments.</p></p>
