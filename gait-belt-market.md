<p><p>The gait belt market is segmented into three primary types: autobuckle, quick release, and side release. Autobuckle gait belts feature a self-locking mechanism that ensures a secure fit while allowing for easy adjustments. Quick release belts enable caregivers to swiftly detach the belt in emergencies, enhancing safety and efficiency. Side release gait belts utilize a buckle positioned on the side for straightforward fastening and unfastening, catering to user comfort and accessibility. Each type caters to specific caregiver and patient needs.</p></p>
