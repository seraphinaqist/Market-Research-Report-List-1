<p><p>The thermal treatment air filtration system market comprises various technologies designed to control and reduce volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and hazardous air pollutants. Thermal oxidizers operate by combusting waste gases to convert pollutants into harmless emissions. Catalytic oxidizers enhance this process using catalysts to lower combustion temperatures. Regenerative thermal oxidizers utilize heat recovery to improve energy efficiency, while regenerative catalytic oxidizers combine this recovery with catalytic processes, maximizing pollutant destruction while minimizing energy consumption. Each type serves specific industrial needs for emissions control.</p></p>
